how it works

From plug-in to insight
in minutes.

A hardware dongle, a mobile app, and a local inference pipeline — no cloud required.

01 / CAPTURE
🔌
Plug in the dongle
The Acty OBD-II hardware connects to your vehicle's diagnostic port and streams real-time PID data over Bluetooth to your phone. 123 parameters captured per session.
BLE · SPP · Real-time
02 / ANALYZE
⚙️
Local ML pipeline
Telemetry runs through on-device models for anomaly detection, predictive maintenance scoring, oil health estimation, and battery health — all processed locally.
Isolation Forest · XGBoost · LSTM
03 / VERIFY
🔏
Tamper-evident reports
Every session report is cryptographically signed with Ed25519. Reports can be independently verified by mechanics, insurers, or buyers — and can't be altered after signing.
Ed25519 · Verifiable · Portable
04 / OWN
🔐
You hold the keys
Your telemetry is encrypted with your key before it ever leaves your device. Acty Labs cannot read your data. No data brokerage. Revenue comes from hardware and verified reports — not you.
Owner-encrypted · Zero-knowledge
features

Built for drivers,
developers, and fleets.

123-PID real-time capture
Full OBD-II PID logging across engine, fuel, emissions, thermal, and electrical systems. Session data is timestamped and indexed for trend analysis across drives.
Predictive maintenance alerts
XGBoost and Random Forest models flag oil change windows, battery degradation, and anomalous sensor patterns before they become failures — trained on your vehicle's own history.
FSM-grounded AI diagnostics
A local RAG pipeline grounds every AI response in your vehicle's factory service manual. Answers are specific to your make, model, and engine — not generic web scraping.
Federated learning — no raw data leaves
Fleet and aggregate insights are derived via Flower federated learning with differential privacy (ε ≤ 1.0) and secure aggregation. Your raw telemetry never touches Acty's servers.
Open API for developers
FastAPI backend with documented endpoints for session data, ML scores, and report generation. Build your own dashboards, integrations, or fleet tooling on top of Acty's pipeline.

privacy architecture

Your car generates
valuable data. Keep it.

Traditional telematics companies make money selling your driving data to insurers, dealers, and data brokers. Acty's business model is hardware and verified reports — not you.

🔑
Owner-encrypted telemetry
All session data is encrypted with your key before leaving your device. Acty Labs holds no decryption capability for your raw telemetry.
🪪
Zero-knowledge identity
Rotating pseudonymous tokens mean your identity is never tied to your driving sessions, even in aggregate analytics.
🧮
Differential privacy
Fleet and population-level model improvements use ε ≤ 1.0 differential privacy and secure aggregation via the Flower framework.
📋
Verifiable signed reports
Ed25519 signatures let mechanics, insurers, or prospective buyers verify a report's authenticity and integrity — independently, without trusting Acty.
🚫
No data brokerage — ever
Acty's revenue comes from hardware sales and verified report fees. Selling your data to third parties is structurally incompatible with our architecture, not just policy.
💻
Local-first inference
ML models run on-device or on your own hardware. Cloud inference is optional and always opt-in. Your driving patterns stay on your hardware by default.
